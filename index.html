Overview

Avat is a digital exchange trading platform for both individuals and institutions. A crypto exchange is a platform on which you can buy and sell cryptocurrencies. You can use exchanges to trade one crypto for another — converting Bitcoin to Litecoin, for example — or to buy crypto using regular currency, like the U.S. Dollar. Exchanges reflect current market prices of the cryptocurrencies they offer. You can also convert cryptocurrencies back into the U.S. Dollar or another currency on an exchange, to leave as cash within your account (if you want to trade back into crypto later) or withdraw to your regular bank account.

Problem

  1. Insecurity
  2. High Trading Fees
  3. Lack Of Crypto Pairs
  4. High Withdrawal Fees
  5. Inconvient User Interface

The Goal

Design Avat’s mobile app to create a simple, instinctive, and memorable experience. Allow users to easily understand the product, buy into the product, ability to trade multiple crypto pairs with low trading fees, provision of safety and security that uses encryption to verify transactions and also transmit cryptocurrency data between wallets and to public ledgers.
